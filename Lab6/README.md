# ARTI308_Lab6

## Ecommerce Customers - Linear Regression Project
In this project, I applied Linear Regression to analyze and predict customer spending for an Ecommerce company. The company sells clothing online but also offers in-store style advice sessions. My goal was to help the company decide whether to focus their development efforts on their Mobile App or their Website based on customer behavior and total yearly spend.

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains information about customers and their interaction with the platform.
I utilized the following features:

-Avg.Session Length: Average time of in-store style advice sessions

-Time on App: Average time spent on the mobile app

-Time on Website: Average time spent on the website

-Length of Membership: How many years the customer has been a member

-Yearly Amount Spent: The total annual spend (Target variable)


## Result
Based on the coefficients calculated by my model, the Length of Membership and Time on App showed the strongest correlation with the amount spent.
I concluded that the company should focus more on their Mobile App rather than the website, as it has a much higher impact on customer spending.
